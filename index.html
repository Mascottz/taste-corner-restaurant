About Taste Corner (Demo)

Taste Corner Restaurant is a demo brand built by Mascott (MasTECH Innovations) to show how a small restaurant in Abuja can look online. The layout is perfect for jollof rice, grills, shawarma, soups and small chops businesses that want to appear more professional.

The idea is simple: give customers a quick view of what you serve, show mouth‑watering pictures, and make it extremely easy for them to call, WhatsApp or visit your location without stress.

Mobile‑first design for customers ordering from their phones.

Sections focused on orders, not just “nice design”.

Easy to adjust colors, fonts and menu items to match any real restaurant.
