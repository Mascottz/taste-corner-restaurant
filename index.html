About Taste Corner

Taste Corner Restaurant is a demo brand built to show how a small restaurant or food spot can present itself online. The layout is perfect for jollof, shawarma, grilled foods, soups and small chops businesses in Abuja.

It gives customers a quick view of your menu, specials, prices and location, with a strong focus on WhatsApp and phone orders – exactly how people like to order in the city.

Friendly & simple

Simple words and clear sections make it easy for any customer to understand what you offer.

Built for orders

Menu sections and call‑to‑action buttons push people to call, WhatsApp or visit – not just look.

Perfect on mobile

Designed first for phone screens where most food searches and orders happen.
